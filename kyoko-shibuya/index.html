Who we are. Okalpha is a concept and detail-focused animation studio based in the middle of nowhere.

After a small lifetime in advertising, we loathe mediocrity and pine for the pretty. We’re proud of keeping things simple: conceptualise engaging ideas and execute them beautifully.
